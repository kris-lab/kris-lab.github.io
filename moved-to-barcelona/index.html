[2020/Q3] Quick update

Last decade (in the world without COVID-19), I have shared my time between Los Angeles, London, Warsaw, Kyiv, and Beijing (see 2017-2020). In the new reality of 2020+, I have decided to move south of Europe and reboot my life. So, I have just moved to Barcelona to continue working on VR/AR/360 projects and additionally focus on fashion tech and PropTech using the newest achievements of researchers from Max Planck Institutes.
